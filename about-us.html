About

Care Skill Academy

The aim of caring is to promote co-operation and integration, leading to the establishment of a highly intensive and extensive care in Nigeria in order to raise the living standards of its peoples, and to maintain and enhance health stability, foster relations among/between patients and contribute to the progress and development in the Health Sector. Improve the lives of people by caring through education, advocacy, research, and connection.
