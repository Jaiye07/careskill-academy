Training Description

Emergency first aid at work (EFAW) training enables a first-aider to give emergency first aid to someone who is injured or becomes ill while at work.

Purpose:

The Emergency First Aid at Work certification aims to:

  • Equip employees with the necessary skills and knowledge to handle emergency situations at work.
  • Ensure that workplaces are prepared to provide immediate care in case of accidents or medical emergencies.
  • Comply with health and safety regulations.

Importance

Having certified first aiders in the workplace is crucial for:

  • Reducing the severity of injuries and potentially saving lives.
  • Enhancing the overall safety culture within the organization.
  • Meeting legal requirements and industry standards.
